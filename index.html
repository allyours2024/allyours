    Why allyours?

    Our project, “allyours,” is different. While traditional co-working spaces and incubators focus broadly on startups and businesses, we specifically target solopreneurs who wish to pursue their own individual projects. These are people with passionate endeavors who have struggled to find a supportive environment. Our focus is not just on providing a workspace but on creating a community that fosters personal and professional growth through peer reviews, workshops, and coaching opportunities.

    About the project

    “allyours” is a co-working space and supports Burmese solopreneurs, aged between 18-35 years, in diverse fields ranging from business to arts and research. Our program enrolls 11 participants per cohort, focusing on sustainable business practices and fostering individual growth over a two-month development phase. There will be weekly tailored workshops, peer feedback sessions, and mentoring programs. At the end of the cohort, participants will showcase their projects, hear out the feedback and implement the projects if selected.

    Mission & Vision

    Mission

    Our mission is to empower solopreneurs by providing comprehensive support through a dedicated co-working space, tailored workshops, and a collaborative community, fostering individual growth and collective advancement.

    Vision

    Our vision is to establish a thriving hub of innovation and growth for Burmese solopreneurs, providing a sustainable environment where they can independently flourish while contributing positively to the community.

    Risk Mangagement Plan

    To manage risks for "allyours," we will implement a comprehensive plan addressing operational, economic, political, financial, market, technological, and reputational risks. We will develop detailed operational procedures, diversify funding sources, and stay informed about political changes. Financial stability will be ensured through robust budgeting and exploring various funding options. Differentiation from competitors will be achieved through unique value propositions, while reliable technology infrastructure and a strong feedback loop will mitigate technological and reputational risks. Regular risk assessments, participant feedback, and an annual review will allow us to adapt and improve our strategies, ensuring the sustainability and success of "allyours."
